Hội An & Đà Nẵng in 4 days

By the OnePlan team·Jun 2026·7 stops · 4 days

Two towns, thirty minutes apart: Hội An's lantern-lit old quarter and Đà Nẵng's long city beach. This four-day route pairs slow mornings with one big finish.

Scroll, and the map follows the whole loop, old town to mountains to coast.

Day 1 · Hội An old town
1

Chùa Cầu & phố cổ

Morning

Begin at the lantern-strung Japanese Covered Bridge and let the old town pull you in, tailors, tea houses and yellow walls made for photos.

2

Chợ đêm An Hội

Evening

Cross the river to the night market for lanterns on the water, grilled everything, and a paper boat with a candle if you're feeling sentimental.

Day 2 · Beach & mountains
3

Biển An Bàng

Morning

A short ride to the calmest beach around, sunbeds, fresh coconut, and nowhere in particular to be.

4

Ngũ Hành Sơn

Afternoon

The Marble Mountains hide caves and pagodas inside; climb for a sea view, then carry on into Đà Nẵng.

Day 3 · Đà Nẵng city
5

Biển Mỹ Khê

Sunrise

Đà Nẵng's long city beach, soft sand and a sunrise worth the early alarm.

6

Cầu Rồng

Evening

Have dinner near the Dragon Bridge; on weekend nights it breathes fire and water at 9pm.

Day 4 · Up to the clouds
7

Cầu Vàng, Bà Nà Hills

Day trip

End big with the Golden Bridge, held up by giant stone hands above the clouds, touristy, yes, and still unforgettable.
