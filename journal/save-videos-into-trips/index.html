How it works

Turn the videos you save into a real trip

By the OnePlan team·June 2026·5 min read

We all do it. A dreamy travel Reel autoplays, you tap save, and you promise yourself you'll go "someday." Then the folder fills up, and someday never comes.

OnePlan Travel exists to close exactly that gap, the one between inspiration and the trip you actually take. Here's how a saved video becomes somewhere you've genuinely been.

Paste a link, get a map

Copy any TikTok or Instagram Reel link, paste it into OnePlan, and the app reads the places in the video and drops them onto your map. The café from that food tour, the viewpoint from that sunset clip, the hotel you screenshotted at 1 a.m., all pinned, all in one place.

The trip stops living in your saved folder and starts living on a map.

Planning a trip of your own? Save places, build the days and split costs in OnePlan, free to start. Get OnePlan →

From scattered saves to one Board

Every traveler has the same mess: a few screenshots here, a note there, a group chat full of links nobody opens. Boards pull it together. Group your pins by city, by vibe, or by trip, and share the Board with whoever's coming along.

Then it plans itself

Once your places live on a map, building the actual days takes minutes. Drag pins into a day-by-day plan, add times and notes, and invite friends so everyone sees the same itinerary, live.

  • Any link, works with TikTok & Instagram
  • Auto-detected places dropped onto your map
  • One tap to save as a Pin or a Board

So stop collecting trips you never take. Paste your next video and watch it turn into a plan.
