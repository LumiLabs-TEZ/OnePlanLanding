5 ready-made itineraries you can steal

By the OnePlan team·June 2026·6 min read

The best trips often start with someone else's plan. There's no shame in it, a good itinerary is meant to be borrowed, tweaked, and made your own.

Inside OnePlan you'll find ready-made plans you can apply to your own trip in a single tap, then reshape however you like. Here are five worth stealing this year.

1. Seoul → Busan · 6 days

A food-and-city run down Korea, palaces and night markets in Seoul, then the KTX south to seafood and beaches in Busan.

2. Da Lat slow trip · 3 days

Pine hills, tiny cafés, and waterfalls. The antidote to a packed schedule, and an easy first trip to plan with friends.

3. Bangkok first-timer · 4 days

Temples in the morning, street food all day, markets at night. Chaotic in the best way, and very easy on the budget.

4. Tokyo highlights · 7 days

Shibuya to Asakusa and everything between, the icons, the side streets, and a day trip when the city gets loud.

5. Hội An & Đà Nẵng · 4 days

Lantern-lit old town, an easy beach, and some of the best food in Vietnam. Slow mornings, golden evenings.

A plan you can steal is a plan you'll actually use.

How to make one yours

Apply a ready-made plan, then swap a café here, add a beach day there, and drop in the places from your own saved videos. In a few minutes you've got something that started as a template and ended up unmistakably your trip.
