Money

Split travel costs without the awkwardness

By the OnePlan team·June 2026·4 min read

Money is the fastest way to ruin a good trip. The dinner someone quietly covered, the cab nobody wrote down, the "I'll get you back later" that never quite happens.

It doesn't have to be like that. Keeping the money clean comes down to three small habits, and letting the app handle the boring part.

Log it as you go

The fix isn't a better spreadsheet at the end of the trip, it's logging costs in the moment. Add who paid for what as it happens, while it's fresh and before anyone's keeping a tense mental tally.

Let the app do the math

OnePlan adds up every expense, works out exactly who owes whom, and nets it all down so the group settles in the fewest possible transfers. No awkward maths at midnight, no one accidentally paying twice.

Split the bill, not the friendship.

Settle up in one tap

  • Split evenly, or by person, on each expense
  • See running balances any time during the trip
  • Settle the whole trip in one tap at the end

Handle the money quietly in the background, and everyone comes home still friends, and maybe still planning the next one.
