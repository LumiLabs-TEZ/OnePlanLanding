Good to know

Is OnePlan free?

Yes, it's free to start and plan your first trips. Some advanced features are part of an optional upgrade.

Which platforms is it on?

OnePlan is available on iOS today. Android is on the way.

Does it work for international trips?

Absolutely. Plan a weekend nearby or a multi-country trip, places, days, and budgets all in one app.

Can I plan with friends?

Yes. Invite anyone to a trip and build the plan together in real time, then split the costs at the end.

How do you split costs on a group trip?

OnePlan tracks who paid for what across the whole trip, then works out who owes whom and settles up for the group in a single tap at the end.
